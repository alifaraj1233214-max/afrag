About EFC

A Legacy of Scientific Innovation & Patient Care

Founded in 1998, EFC (Excellence in Pharmaceutical Care) has grown from a pioneering research lab into one of the world's most trusted pharmaceutical companies. Our mission is simple: develop breakthrough treatments that transform lives, backed by rigorous science and compassionate care.

With state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities operating under the highest international standards, we deliver pharmaceutical solutions across oncology, cardiology, neurology, infectious diseases, and rare conditions — serving healthcare providers and patients in over 180 countries.

Innovation First Cutting-edge R&D with 15% annual revenue reinvested
Patient-Centered Every decision guided by patient outcomes and safety
Global Impact Accessible medicines across emerging and developed markets
